What is glossAPI?

GlossAPI is an open-source toolkit created by GFOSS that converts raw Greek text — literature, legislation, science and culture — into clean structured and intelligent data.

Whether you are training language models, building a smarter search, or exploring the Greek digital heritage, GlossAPI provides you with the tools and data sets to build open, ethical, and scalable products.

Get involved and join the growing community of researchers, developers and digital humanists using GlossAPI to ensure the future of Greek language in AI.
